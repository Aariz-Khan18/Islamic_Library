About Islam & Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ)

Islam is a religion of peace, submission, and complete obedience to the Will of Allah. It is built upon five pillars: Declaration of Faith (Shahada), Prayer (Salah), Charity (Zakat), Fasting (Sawm), and Pilgrimage (Hajj).


Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) is the final messenger of Allah, sent as a mercy to all of creation (Rahmat lil-'Alamin). His life, character, and teachings are the perfect example for all of humanity to follow. "Indeed, in the Messenger of Allah you have an excellent example for whoever has hope in Allah and the Last Day, and remembers Allah often." (Quran 33:21)

About Ilm (Knowledge)

The first revelation was “Iqra” (Read). Seeking knowledge is obligatory upon every Muslim. Knowledge elevates, purifies and strengthens faith. "Allah will exalt in degree those of you who believe, and those who have been granted knowledge." (Quran 58:11)

📖 What is the Noble Quran?

The Noble Quran is the literal, uncreated word of Allah (SWT) revealed to Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) through the Angel Jibril (Gabriel) over a period of 23 years. It is the ultimate source of guidance, law, and healing for humanity.

Hadith Section (Kutub al-Sittah)

📜 What is Hadith?

Hadith represents the documented sayings, actions, and silent approvals of Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ). While the Quran is the word of Allah, the Hadith provides the practical application, detailed explanation, and context for the Quranic teachings.
